Projects

1. Autonomous Driving Robot

Worked with a team @CatholicUniv to develop an autonomous robot using NVIDIA Jetson Nano board and Raspberry Pi Camera.

The robot is capable of avoiding obstacles, keeping track of the lane lines, and recognizing determined traffic signs.

Applied transfer learning to pre-trained deep learning architecture to optimize the system performance.

Deep Learning Frameworks used: PyTorch, TensorFlow.

2. Home assessment of grasp development in infants at risk for fine motor delay

Assessed spontaneous hand use using video capture (Intel Realsense D435) and interactive grasp force with instrumented toys in infants.

Implemented different Deep Learning approaches: DeepLabCut (mainly), OpenPose, Deep High Resolution HRNet.

Signaled potential avenues for early interventions to encourage object exploration and functional hand use.
